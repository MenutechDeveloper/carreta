Our Story

Authentic Mexican Flavors

La Carreta Restaurant has been serving authentic Mexican cuisine to the Houston community. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the true flavors of Mexico.

Our name "La Carreta" (The Cart) pays homage to the traditional food carts found throughout Mexico, bringing authentic street food flavors to your table.

Our Philosophy

At La Carreta, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients, prepared with traditional techniques. Our chefs bring years of experience and a passion for Mexican culinary traditions to every dish we serve.
